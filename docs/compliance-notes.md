# Compliance Notes

This project starts as a general-purpose groupware foundation. It should not claim compliance with public-sector, healthcare, or privacy standards until the relevant deployment environment, controls, and operating procedures have been audited.

## Japan-Focused Review Areas

- Personal data handling and retention rules
- Access control and account lifecycle
- Audit log preservation
- Incident response procedure
- Accessibility testing
- Backup location and data residency
- Vendor and subcontractor management
- Closed-network operation requirements

## Healthcare Boundary

The first healthcare target is staff operations such as notices, schedules, facilities, and internal approvals. Clinical information workflows must be designed as a separate regulated module.
